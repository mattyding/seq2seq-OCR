protective clothing forminersa campaign for inducing miners to wear protective clothing is spreading though south yorkshire collieries for some years the miners reluctance to the wearing of hare hats or safety helmets has been steadily overcome and one firm doncaster amalgarnated collieries limited has issued s safety helmets free of charge to miners in their employ and they hope in time that all the  miners at their six collieries will wear them official statistics are now available to prove that in hundreds of accidents which might have proved fatal miners caught by falling coal have escaped with bruises while lighter falls of coal and debris have no effect whatever when the safety helmets are worn some colliery companies are now issuing safety boots to their men while safety goggles and kneecaps the latter for those whose work entails hours of kneeling are also to be introduced
