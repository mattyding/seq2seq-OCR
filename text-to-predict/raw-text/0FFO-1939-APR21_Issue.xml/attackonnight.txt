attack on night watchmanscotland yard detectives were trying yesterday to trace two men who are alleged to have attacked a night watchman named george baunder who works for a firm in holloway n baunder told the police that noticing a fire had started in a small shed belonging to messrs g cole limited timber merchants in manor gardens whose premises are next door to the building in which he works he went to investigate and was met by two men who came from the direction of the shed one of the men as he ran struck baunder in the face with a piece of wood and both then made their escape baunders right cheek was injured but after treatment at the royal northern hospital he was able to go home the fire was overcome by firemen without much difficulty being restricted to the shed and the area immediately round it
