airmen rescued inchanneldescent in fogfrom our oxvn correspondentparis may a message from dieppe states that a british percival gull aeroplane registra tion mark gacjr with two persons on board fell into the channel early last evening and that the occupants whose names are given as mr gazdar of bombay and herr hell of vienna were rescued and taken to dieppe by a french trawlerit appears that the pilot lost his way in a thick fog and decided to land as petrol was running short when he realized that he was over the sea he flew close to the trawler and made signs of distress whereupon the crew of the vessel launched a lifeboat ready to rescue the two men as soon as the machine alighted herr hell was thrown out as the aeroplane touched the water and both men were bruised but not seriously hurt
