turkish byelectionfrom our own corespondentlconstantinople dec the constantinople byelection vill probably be held on thursday but neither the popular party nor the progressists have yet officially announced their candidates though it is practically certain that hakki chinasi pasha formerly a naval surgeon will be the government candidateseveral deputies are comning from angora to speak on behalf of the government and it is said that great importance is attached to this election as the bulk of the  electors who  months ago voted for the popular party candidates still remain and as the party leaders profess to have received assurances that they will again vote for the popular party it is hard to see why such importance is being attached to what is apparently a foregone conclusion on the other hand it is thought by many persons that the pro gressists and independents stand better chances in the provinces
