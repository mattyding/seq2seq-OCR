 31
 - The authorities of the far-famed seat of learning at this place having received official intelligence of Her Majesty the Queen Dowager's wish to visit the school on this day, proper preparations were made
 Her Majesty arrived about 20 minutes to 3 o'clock, and proceeded at once with her numerous suite to the Speech Room, where the scholars were assembled
 Here Her Majesty begged an extra week's holyday for the school at Christmas, which was of course granted
 After inspecting the Monitor's library and other parts of the building, Her Majesty left for her residence, Staumore Priory, amid the loyal and grateful cheers of upwards of 300 Harrovians
