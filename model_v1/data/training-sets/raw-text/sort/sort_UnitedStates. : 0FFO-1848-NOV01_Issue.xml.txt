 (BY ELECTRIC TELEGRAPH
) ARRIVAL OF THE CAMBRIA
 The British and North American Company's steamer Cambria has arrived
 She left Boston on the 18th inst
, and brings 67 passengers, but no specie
 The Britannia arrived out on the 17th inst
 The State of Pennsylvania has surprised both parties by inclining to the Whig candidate; whilst Ohio, which was relied upon as a Whig State, has positively chosen its Governor from the other party
 The official returns have not, however, yet been received from either State
